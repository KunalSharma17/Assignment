How to Join


I) Commissioned Officers

Commissioned Officers consists of the following ranks:- Lieutenant, Captain, Major, Lt. Colonel, Colonel, Brigadier, Major General, Lt. General & General.

  • National Defence Academy
    Age : 16.5 -19.5 Years
    Qualifications - 10+2

  • Indian Military Academy (Non Tech)
    Age : 19-24 Years
    Qualifications - Graduate

  • Officers Training Academy (Tech Men & Women)
    Age : 19-27 Years
    Qualifications - Graduate

  • NCC Special (Men & Women)
    Age : 19-25 Years
    Qualifications - Graduate 50% aggregrate "A" or "B" grade in NCC "C" certificate

  • TGC(Education)
    Age : 23-27 Years
    Qualifications - MA/MSc in 1st or 2nd division

  • UES
    Age : 18-24 Years
    Qualifications - Pre Final Student of Engineering Degree Course

  • TGC
    Age : 21-27 Years
    Qualifications - BE/B.Tech streams of Engineering/N.Arch MSc computer

  • JAG (Men & Women)
    Age : 21-27 years
    Qualifications - Law Graduate LLB with 55% marks Registered with Bar Council of India/State

  • 10+2 Tech
    Age : 15.5-19.5 Years
    Qualifications - 10+2 (70% Marks with PCM)

II) Junior Commissioned Officers

Junior Commissioned Officers consists of the following ranks:- Subedar Major, Subedar & Naib Subedar.

  • Catering JCO (ASC)
    Age : 21-27 Years
    Qualifications - 10+2 with Diploma in Hotel Management & Catering Technology

  • Religious Teacher (JCO)
    Age : 27-34 Years
    Qualifications - Graduate + He is qualified in his religion

III) Non Commissioned Officers

Non Commissioned Officers consists of the following ranks:- Sepoy, Naik, Lance Naik & Havildar.

  • Havildar (Education)
    Age : 21-25 Years
    Qualifications - For Group X - MA/MSC/MCA or BA/BSC/BCA with BEd BSC(IT) for Group Y - BSC/BA/BCA(IT) without BEd

  • Surveyor Auto Carto (Engineers)
    Age : 20-25 Years
    Qualifications - BA/BSC with Maths 10+2 (Math & Science)

  • Soldier General Duty
    Age : 17.5-21 Years
    Qualifications - 10th pass with 45% & each subject must contain 33%

  • Soldier Clerk/Store Keeper
    Age : 17.5-23 Years
    Qualifications - 10+2 pass (60% marks) (Each subject must have 50% marks) with Maths & English

  • Solider Tech
    Age : 17.5-23 Years
    Qualifications - 10+2 pass (50% marks) (Each subject must have 40% marks) with Physics, Chemistry, Maths

  • Soldier Nursing Assistant/Tech (RVC)
    Age : 17.5-23 Years
    Qualifications - 10+2 pass (50% marks( (Each subject must have 40$ marks) with Physics, Chemistry, Biology

  • Soldier Tradesman
    Age : 17.5-23 Years
    Qualifications - 10th/ITI and 8th pass (for some trades)
