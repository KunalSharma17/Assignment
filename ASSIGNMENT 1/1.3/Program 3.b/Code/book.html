15 Reasons Reading Is The Best Hobby Ever

  1. You visit a different world every time you read a book.
  2. It makes you live the life of a protagonist.
  3. When you're reading, you gain from the knowledge and experiences of someone else.
  4. Reading fiction often puts you in different places and situations, making you a lot more imaginative!
  5. Reading makes you empathetic because you gain a better understanding of people and the world.
  6. Books offer the best company when you’re travelling.
  7. Reading slows down your heart rate, and eases tension in the muscles.
  8. On lonely days, reading gives you solace and acts like a best friend.
  9. Because book markets are where your heart lies!
  10. And book cafes are where you’d love to give away your heart.
  11. Your vast book collection offers you a wealth of information Google can't exactly provide.
  12. Thanks to technology, reading has even become an inexpensive hobby.
  13. As a consequence of habit, a reader has several stories to share, making them not only an interesting person, but also a great conversationalist.
  14. Your vocabulary expands manifold.
  15. Reading is the only thing in the world that makes time travel possible.
